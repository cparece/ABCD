Manage Subscriptions

Group Name Description Email
Announcements ABCD Announce is a mailing list for posting subgroup meeting announcements. ABCD members who do not wish to join a lot of mailing lists can subscribe to abcd-announce to be informed of subgroup meetings. email
Bioinformatics The ABCD Bioinformatics group concerns itself with the intersection of computation and biology. email
BRIO This list is for discussion of the BRIO query tool email
Cloud Computing This group will undertake anything cloud at Harvard and is open to all the schools, students, faculty, staff, ... This group will be a forum to share information across Harvard. email
Cluster Computing The Cluster Computing group provides a forum to explore the issues and interests surrounding Cluster Computing. These are fairly broad issues and interests that may span other ABCD groups as well. email
Realtime Communications This group is for anyone (in any role at Harvard) interested in: Learning about marketing/communications initiatives and resources, Pooling knowledge about concepts, best practices, tools, and vendors, Building a network of marketing professionals, etc. email
Digital Asset Management Systems This group will meet to determine what types of digital asset management systems are needed or in use throughout the university and how we can capitalize on our common requirements. email
DEC/Compaq The DEC group is a holdover from the days when Harvard and its affiliates were primarily a Digital shop. Through the years this has changed, however, there is still a significant amount of Compaq (aka DEC) hardware and software in active use. email
Graphic Design This group will be concerned with graphic design for print and the web, user experience, marketing, interactive design, and information architecture.
Digital Publishing The Harvard Digital Publishing Collaborative is an ABCD staff affinity group with a focus on how content is authored, produced, secured, and delivered across web- and device-based content platforms.
Technology in Education The Technology in Education working group is a Harvard staff affinity group whose focus is on innovative uses of technology in teaching and learning.
Email The Email working group addresses issues pretaining to supporting e-mail within the University and affiliates. email
Engineering The ABCD Engineering Working Group is a forum where engineers working at Harvard and the affiliated institutions, that design and develop hardware, can share ideas and expertise. email
